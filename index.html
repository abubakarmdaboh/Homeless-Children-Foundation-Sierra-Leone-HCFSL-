HISTORY OF THE ORGANIZATION

Homeless Children Foundation Sierra Leone (HCFSL) was found by ABU BAKA M. DABOH. He is a Sierra Leonean and a strong hardworking man in the field of advocacy. He was able to move ahead with most of his dream work with another strong lady by the name of Katumu k. Ngebuva who has as well served many organizations in the field of advocacy. With the two of them, Abu Bakar M. Daboh was able to register the organization as a Non-Governmental Organization on the 28th July 2021. The both knew it was time to set the BOARD OF DIRECTORS after the registration was done at Ministry of Social Welfare. On the 08th August,2021 Abu Bakar called on a general meeting for all intrested members to attend. Other people were invited from other different social groups and organizations to be part of the meeting. They were able to do the seletion of the BOARD by asking questions relating to leadership skills through What'sApp voice mail. They also used the intelligency of some people even do they might have little knownledge in adcocacy but how best the organization will grow if they are empowered. Brief description of the Organization;

Homeless Children Foundation Sierra Leone [HCFSL] functions as a Non-Governmental Organization which aims at to call on the attention of the Government of the day not to forget about the vulnerable, less privileged and homeless children to be treated equally and get access to Government and private donations and some other basic needs nationwide. [HCFSL] also extend services to youths and adults in their various communities. This fight has started way back in 2018 but was officially registered at Social Welfare Gender and Children affair as a functionary Organization in 2019. In the year 2019-2020 was when we had serious covid-19 out brake which stopped most working activities in our country; as a result, staffs were using WhatsApp group to communicate information. Online meetings were also done through the WhatsApp group. It was during that time the Board of Directors were elected. In the past year 2021, [HCFSL] embarked on many forms of advocacy programs to reach out communities with victims living there as their place of locations. Our aims were to impower, give them circus social counselling and make them believe the process of over coming their various situations. Through the field of Advocacy, [HCFSL] has been able to achieve the objectives of serving the masses more especially when it comes to young youths in respect to their attitudes, behaviors, and social reaction towards their youth age. Today we are proud to say we have successful delivery in all our past proposals through the relentless working efforts of the Board of Directors. The help of [HCFSL] talented staffs has also brought our organization remarkable results through their field and office duties credible track records of experience in dealing with youths and communities.

How do we (HCFSL) cartigorized homeless?

The word Homeless is an umbrella term which entails people with no home, people living on the street, volnerable people and even person host to another in respect to problem of sleeping accomodating. Under high level of research will tell you that person living under the system of rent is also a homeless person because that person do not have a fix address untill he/she is owning a structure on-top of his head were he can get his basic needs around.

Problems foced by homeless in Sierra Leone;

Children:

Children have much issues to face in their education. Because of no fixed address they tend to change schools any time the famile move to another location for setttlement. Their friendship line keep changing all the time which can lead them to bad behavour.

Youths:

Youths are in big problem when it comes to being homeless. As a result of no fixed address some will decide to join friends who are having structure over their heads.

Parents:

Most parent has little or no money to catter for the entire family through out the year. The little at hand will be given for rent and lefted with no money.
